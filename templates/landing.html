Questions

Which devices does this work with? +

CyberQ Dashboard works with any BBQ Guru CyberQ Cloud device. You'll need your FlameBoss/BBQ Guru login credentials (same as myflameboss.com) and your Device ID to connect.

Where do I find my Device ID? +

Log in to myflameboss.com, go to your device, and the Device ID is shown in the URL and on the device settings page. It's typically a 6-digit number.

Do I need to install anything? +

No. CyberQ Dashboard runs entirely in your browser. Works on iPhone, Android, Mac, PC — any device with a browser.

Is my BBQ Guru login safe? +

Your credentials are encrypted and stored securely. We only use them to poll the FlameBoss API on your behalf — the same calls your device makes to the cloud.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. No questions, no hassle.
